Built for people who care about movies

MoviWebApp is a free, open-source film tracker. No algorithms pushing blockbusters at you. No ads. Just your list, your taste, and your people.

📋

Track everything you watch

Add films, rate them, mark your watchlist. Import your full history from Letterboxd in seconds.

👥

Find your people

Taste match scores show exactly how much your film taste overlaps with others. Follow people who love what you love.

🌙

Movie Nights

Plan group watches, suggest films, vote on the pick, and share a shareable result card when the winner is declared.

✍️

Write & read reviews

Short takes or long essays — write what you actually think. See what your community thinks before you watch.

🏆

Challenges & streaks

Set watching goals, track your year in film, and see your stats at a glance in your annual recap.

🔓

Free & open source, forever

No paywalls. No premium tiers. The code is on GitHub — read it, fork it, contribute to it.

The story

MoviWebApp started as a weekend project — a simple list app for keeping track of what to watch next. It grew into something we actually wanted to use every day: a place to log films, discover what friends were watching, and settle the eternal group-chat debate of "what should we watch tonight?"

We believe film tracking should be simple, beautiful, and social — without dark patterns or paywalls. If you love it, buy us a coffee. If you want to help build it, open a PR.
