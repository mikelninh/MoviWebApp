Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

We collect only what's needed to run the service:

  • Username and password — your password is hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plain text.
  • Films, ratings, and reviews — the content you add to your list.
  • Follow relationships — who you follow and who follows you.

We do not collect your email address, real name, location, or any payment information. We do not run advertising or sell data.

Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in. No tracking cookies, no third-party analytics.

Film metadata

When you add a film, we look up its poster, director, and plot from the OMDb API. Your username is not sent to OMDb — only the film title and year.

Your data

You can download all your data as a CSV at any time. To delete your account, contact us via GitHub Issues and we'll remove it promptly.

Open source

The entire application is open source on GitHub. You can read exactly how your data is stored and used. There are no hidden trackers.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll note it at the top of this page with a new date. We'll never retroactively change how existing data is used without notice.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue on GitHub or reach out via Ko-fi.
